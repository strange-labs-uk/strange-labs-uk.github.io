Strange Labs

Making things that serve people, not the algorithm

I build things for people who suspect that something important has been lost in our seemingly endless quest for progress. In a world optimised for engagement, convenience, and consumption, I explore things that push back - apps that help you reconnect with the people and experiences that matter, tools that question decision-making and whether "progress" always points in the right direction. I want you to think more mindfully, to use technology with intention rather than be used by it.

Everything here starts from the same place: technology should serve human life, not replace it. Progress isn't bad, but perhaps its definition is.

Sometimes I just make something for fun.
