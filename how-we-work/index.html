Our Custom PC Building Process

Ordering a PC with Strange Labs is a simple, but personal service. We work hand in hand with you to create your perfect PC. This means, rather than offering off the shelf configurations, we tailor your PC using current, highly rated and specifically chosen components.

1. Personal Consultation

Every Strange Labs computer is custom-built to match your exact needs. Instead of choosing from pre-made configurations, you’ll work directly with our team to create your perfect PC. Whether you’re a tech enthusiast who knows exactly what you want, or someone looking for expert guidance, we’re here to help.

2. Design & Specification

  • Submit your requirements through our online order form, telling us how you plan to use your PC
  • Our team carefully selects premium components that match your needs and budget
  • We present a detailed specification for your review
  • We refine the design together until you’re completely satisfied

4. Build Process (3-5 Working Days)

Our master engineers create your PC with meticulous attention to detail:

  • Premium components sourced from trusted suppliers
  • Precise assembly with professional cable management
  • Comprehensive benchmarking and stress testing
  • Temperature and performance optimization
  • Quality assurance checks at every step

In the rare case of a faulty component, we’ll replace it immediately, though this may add 3-5 working days to the build time.

5. Delivery & Setup

Once your PC passes our rigorous testing:

  • Next-day delivery service or collection
  • Secure, protective packaging
  • Tracking details provided via email
  • Setup instructions included
  • Please retain packaging until you’ve verified everything is working perfectly

Our commitment to quality doesn’t end with delivery. If you encounter any issues during transit or setup, contact us immediately for support through any of our usual channels.
