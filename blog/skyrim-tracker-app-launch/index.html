The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim - 100% Completion Tracker

Posted: Wednesday, August 20, 2025
Last updated: Wednesday, August 20, 2025
Author: Strange Labs

Introduction

After hundreds of hours exploring the vast world of Tamriel, have you ever wondered if you’ve truly seen everything Skyrim has to offer? Whether you’re a veteran Dragonborn or just starting your adventure, achieving a true 100% completion in Skyrim is one of gaming’s most ambitious undertakings.

The Scope of Skyrim Completion

Skyrim is massive. With over 250 quests, hundreds of locations to discover, countless books to read, and achievements spanning multiple DLCs, keeping track of what you’ve completed is nearly impossible.

Even if you complete every single quest in the game, you are still only about 5% of the way through true 100% completion. The remaining 95% consists of collecting items, discovering locations, mastering skills, and encountering every possible interaction in the game world.

What Is Tracked?

Our tracker covers 40+ comprehensive categories with over 11,000 individual items:

Collections

  • Locations (453) - All discoverable locations and dungeons
  • Attire (2572) - All clothing and armor pieces
  • Books (768) - All books and skill books
  • Collectibles (287) - Unique items and collectibles
  • Ingredients (109) - All alchemy ingredients
  • Items (1131) - Miscellaneous items
  • Keys (324) - All keys collected
  • Property (18) - Houses and properties
  • Treasure Maps (12) - All treasure maps
  • Weapons (3070) - All weapons and crafted items

Enhancements and Effects

  • Alchemy Effects (732) - All effects learned
  • Diseases (9) - All diseases contracted/cured

  • Enchanting Effects (58) - All enchanting effects

Community Foundation

This tracker builds upon the meticulous work of dedicated Skyrim players who’ve mapped every corner of the game over more than a decade. It incorporates data from the UESP wikipedia, along with community resources like the Skyrim 100% Completion Checklist and Savior of Skyrim - Master Spreadsheet, representing hundreds of hours of research by passionate players.
